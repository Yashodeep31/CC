What is Wordpress : 

WordPress is the simplest, most popular way to create your own website or 
blog. In fact, WordPress powers over 43.3% of all the websites on the Internet.

WordPress is an open-source content management system (CMS). It’s a popular 
tool for individuals without any coding experience who want to build websites 
and blogs. The software doesn’t cost anything. Anyone can install, use, and 
modify it for free.
